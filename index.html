Call of Duty; My Story

Call of Duty

Best game in the world

Call of Duty has been the one single game I've played since I was 12 years old. The main reason why It totally caugth me was because it's a rather competitive game. Not only do I enjoy playing it with my friends but also alone, because it has the league mode modality.

I'd recommend everybody play this great game. The more you play it, the more you get addicted to it (in the good way).

The game modes I enjoy playing the most are:

  • Domination
  • Hardpoint
  • Team Deathmatch

Call of Duty besides having multiplayer, also has this thing called zombies mode which people like a lot (personally I don't). This is a great alternative for whenever you get bored of playing regular multiplayer matches.
